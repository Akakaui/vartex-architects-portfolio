# Google My Business: Client Handover & Manager Setup

This guide is for the **Vartex Architects Team**. To ensure your business ranks #1 on Google Search and Maps in Lagos and Enugu, please follow these steps to set up your profile and grant the technical team access to optimize it.

---

## 🔒 Security First: No Passwords Needed
To keep your account secure, **do not share your Gmail password.** Google allows you to add "Managers" to your business profile who can handle the setup, photos, and keywords for you while you remain the sole owner.

---

## Step 1: Create or Claim Your Business Profile
1.  Go to [google.com/business](https://google.com/business) and sign in with your main firm email (Gmail).
2.  Search for **"Vartex Architects"**. 
    - If it already exists, click **"Claim this business."**
    - If not, select **"Add your business to Google."**
3.  Follow the prompts to enter your address (use your Lagos or Enugu office location).
4.  **Verification**: Google will likely ask to send a postcard to your office. Once it arrives (usually 1-2 weeks), it will have a 5-digit code. **Keep this code safe and notify your developer when it arrives.**

---

## Step 2: Add Developer as a "Manager"
Once your profile is set up, follow these steps to let us handle the technical optimization:

1.  In your Google Business Profile dashboard, click the **three dots (⋮ menu)** in the top right corner.
2.  Select **"Business Profile settings."**
3.  Click on **"People and access."**
4.  Click **"Add"** (plus icon).
5.  Enter the Developer's email address: **[INSERT DEVELOPER EMAIL HERE]**
6.  Select the role **"Manager"**.
7.  Click **"Invite"**.

*Note: As a "Manager," the developer can upload project photos, update keywords, and respond to reviews, but they CANNOT delete the profile or change ownership.*

---

## Step 3: What We Need From You
To make your profile look premium and professional, please provide:
- **10+ High-Resolution Photos**: Exterior and interior shots of your best projects.
- **Business Hours**: Your standard office operation times.
- **Official Phone**: The number you want clients to call from the Search result.

---

## Why This Matters
When someone in Nigeria searches for **"Top Architect Lagos"** or **"Vertex Architects Enugu"**, Google looks at three things:
1.  **Proximity**: How close you are to them.
2.  **Relevance**: How well your GMB profile matches their search (our job to optimize).
3.  **Prominence**: Your reviews and photos (your job to generate).

By following this guide, we can ensure Vartex Architects is the first firm they see.
