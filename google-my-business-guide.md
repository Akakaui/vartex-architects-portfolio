# Google My Business: Local SEO Setup Guide

This guide is designed for **Vartex Architects** to establish and rank their local presence on Google Search and Maps.

## 1. Getting Started
1.  Go to [google.com/business](https://google.com/business) and sign in with the firm's official Gmail account.
2.  Search for **"Vartex Architects"**. If it doesn't appear, select "Add your business to Google".

## 2. Business Information
- **Business Name**: `Vartex Architects`
- **Business Category**: 
    - *Primary*: `Architect`
    - *Additional*: `Interior Designer`, `Architectural Designer`
- **Description**: *"Vartex Architects is a multidisciplinary architecture studio specializing in sustainable, modern residential and commercial designs across Nigeria."*

## 3. Location & Service Area
- **Address**: Enter the physical office address (if applicable).
- **Service Areas**: Add `Lagos` and `Enugu`. This tells Google you serve both regions even if you only have one office.

## 4. Verification (Crucial)
Google will ask to verify the business.
- **Method**: Usually via a **Postcard** sent to the physical address.
- **Step**: Once the postcard arrives (1-2 weeks), enter the 5-digit code in your GMB dashboard.
- **Note**: Do not change your address or name during the verification process.

## 5. Boosting Your Ranking (Local SEO)
To show up first when people search "Architect near me":

1.  **Add High-Quality Photos**: Upload at least 10 photos of your best projects.
2.  **Collect Reviews**: Ask your best clients to leave a 5-star review. **Reviews are the #1 way to rank high in the map pack.**
3.  **Accuracy**: Ensure the phone number and address match exactly what is on your website.

## 6. Website Link
Ensure your website link is set to:
`https://vartexarchitects.com`
